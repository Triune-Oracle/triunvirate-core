Why It Works

Reduced Hallucinated Agency
By enforcing strict role boundaries, agents are prevented from assuming authority they do not possess, ensuring actions remain grounded.
Preserved Accountability
The DSP loop ensures every decision is recorded, creating a permanent and verifiable audit trail for all system actions.
Safe Scaling
The framework allows for the deployment of multiple agent swarms without the risk of uncontrolled autonomy escalation.
Separation of Vision from Execution
Strategic intent is decoupled from tactical implementation, allowing for clear governance and oversight at every level.
