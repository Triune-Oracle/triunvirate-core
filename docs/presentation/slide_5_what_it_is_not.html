What It Is Not

Not a Persuasion Engine
DSP is a cognitive process standard, not a technology designed for behavioral manipulation or psychological influence.
Not a Guarantee System
The framework acknowledges execution risk; all outputs are probabilistic and do not constitute deterministic performance guarantees.
Not a Financial Prediction Model
While it manages value rails, the system does not predict market movements or offer financial advice of any kind.
Not Autonomous Without Oversight
The Triumvirate operates under human-defined constraints and requires directive authority from the Oracle role.
